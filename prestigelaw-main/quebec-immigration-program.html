Quebec Immigration Program

Quebec is located in eastern Canada where most commonly spoken and the official language is French. The province has a unique status in Canada, with rich history, culture and offers a unique blend of French and Canadian heritage. The province preserves certain specific provisions and powers to protect and promote its distinct culture and language while being part of the Canadian federal system. The two of the most vigorous and popular cities are Montreal and Quebec City, the capital, is where most people tend to call home when they immigrate to Quebec, Canada
